Favorites

My favorite hobbies:

  • Playing sports - I used to train basketball for 8 years and played professionally for a couple of years for my home town club. Besides basketball, in my free time I love to play and watch football and handball.
  • Playing a guitar - I was always impressed with the beauty of musical instruments, and a few years ago I started learning how to play a guitar. It's still on of my favorite things to do, especially while taking a break from studying, because it relaxes me and clears my mind! If u want to learn how to play, I highly recommend Ultimate guitar, it helped me a lot!
  • Listening to music - I'm a big fan of rock and rap music. My favorite rock bands are Deep Purple, Nirvana and Pink Floyd. My favorite rappers are J Cole and Kendrick Lamar.
  • Watcing movies and series - My favorite things to watch are anime and sci-fi movies. If you're interested in this kind of stuff I highly recommend Samurai Champloo (my favorite anime) or Interstellar (my favorite sci-fi movie), you won't regret it :)
